Our Story

Three Decades of Hospitality in the Heartland

Legacy Hotel opened its doors on Aba Road in 1996, the first of its kind to bring international standards of comfort to Owerri. What began as a single wing of twenty rooms has grown into one of Imo State's most trusted addresses for business, celebration, and rest.

Every detail, from the carved mahogany reception desk to the courtyard gardens, is maintained as it was built: by hand, with patience, and with the next generation of guests in mind.
